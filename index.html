James Bolton

James Bolton (born 27 February) is a British racing driver currently competing in Formula One.

He made his virtual Formula One debut on a PlayStation in 2010 and, driving for Scuderia Ferrari, won his first two races in the category. He went on to win the championship in his first season. With nine FIA Formula 1 World Championship titles, he is the most successful driver in the sport's history.

Bolton competes on circuits all over the world from the comfort of his sitting room, where he reaches speeds of literally zero miles an hour and walks away unharmed from any accidents that happen 'on track'.
